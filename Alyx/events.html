  • 1017 ALYX 9SM Opens Toronto Pop-Up With Exclusive Nike Products Nov 18, 2019 from 1:00pm - 3:00pm
    After launching pop-ups in Paris, Matthew Williams and 1017 ALYX 9SM continue to expand their reach, now opening a pop-up space in Toronto. Located at 26 Bellair Street, the interior of the 1,695-square-foot temporary store is designed and constructed by OKRM and Hesselbrand. ALYX's Toronto pop-up houses the brand's SS19 collection, in addition to exclusive products and collaborations including Nike Essentials, Nike MMW 002 collection and select items from Dior SS19. Following this past weekend's launch, the 1017 ALYX 9SM Toronto space is open through June. You can get a look inside the pop-up via the gallery above. 1017 ALYX 9SM Toronto Pop-Up 26 Bellair St. Toronto, ON M5R 2C7, Canada

  • ALYX + VIBRAM WORKSHOP October 20, 2019 from 2:00pm - 3:30pm
    This workshop will focus on the idea of designing moveable and durable shoes. The conversation will buildout the thought process of understanding what is needed to build durable fashion. Touchpoints will also include the importance of understanding the material that is used to construct activewear and the connection it has with human anatomy.
