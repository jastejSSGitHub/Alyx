Overview

Intro

ALYX a foundation built on the undercurrent. Designed by Matthew Williams, the brand is built on the concept of high quality materials mixed with subversive culture. Based in New York City and produced in Italy, Matthew combines influences from his upbringing in California and New York with his commitment to detail, innovation and desire to push new ideas forward. A mix of slimline tailoring, lux fabrics, graphic jersey and aggressively elegant accessories offers a complete wardrobe and a new perspective.
